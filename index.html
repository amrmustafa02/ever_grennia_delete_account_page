How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account and all related data (including your cart, orders, and preferences), please follow the steps below:

  1. Open the app and go to your profile page.
  2. Scroll down and select the "Delete Account" option.
  3. Confirm the deletion by following the prompts.
  4. Once confirmed, all your data will be permanently deleted, including any saved orders and preferences.
